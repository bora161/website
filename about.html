About BISMUN

The BISMUN is Bonn International School's Model United Nations conference, with over 100 participants each year. BISMUN is attended by students in grades 9 to 12, and is an educational and rewarding experience. If you are a student outside of BIS, we strongly encourage you to join a local MUN community. For students in Bonn, the BISMUN organization invites you to join our Community, which hosts regular workshops and events to help students prepare for MUN conferences.

To get into contact, feel free to send us an email at: bismun.contact@gmail.com
