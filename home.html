What is Model United Nations

Model United Nations (MUN) is an academic simulation in which students take on the role of delegates representing different countries and debate global issues in a formal conference setting.

Participants develop public speaking, research, diplomacy, and critical thinking skills while learning about international relations and global cooperation. To the right is a video made by a seasoned BISMUN delegate that provides a brief introduction to MUN.
